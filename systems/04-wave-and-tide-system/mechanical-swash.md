# Mechanical Swash

## Current Mechanism

- Drive: belt-driven Nema 23 stepper assembly.
- Linear rail: SFC16-1000 mm dual-rail slide.
- Belt: 2GT, 15 mm width.
- Chamber: 4 in PVC pipe, 24 in length.
- Chamber coupling: Fernco XL 3 in flexible pipe coupler.
- Motion: the stepper moves the rail vertically, moving the chamber up and down and pumping water in and out of the Seagrass Meadow biome.
- Vent: opposite end of chamber is vented and returns to the system.
- Housings/gears: 3D-printed PETG.

## Design Constraint

No mechanical devices, metal, or electronics are permitted inside the glass habitat. All wave/tide motion is generated externally.
