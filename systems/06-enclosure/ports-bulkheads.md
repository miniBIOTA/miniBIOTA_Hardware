# Ports And Bulkheads

## Modular Port Architecture

Each biome has:

- Two left ports.
- Two right ports.
- One bottom port.
- Three top ports.
- Port diameter: 3 in.

Ports are permanently drilled bulkheads.

## Couplers And Rigid Sections

Connections use:

- 3 in Fernco rubber PVC couplers.
- Rigid PVC sections.
- Bottom, top, and side routing depending on hydrology and air topology.

## Service Implication

The system is modular and expandable, but rearrangement requires draining, disassembly, and resealing.
